\begin{answer}

Observing a positive partial label $y^{(i)}$ = 1 tells us for sure the hidden true label is 1 then: 

\begin{eqnarray*}
p(y^{(i)} =1\mid x^{(i)}) &=& p(y^{(i)} = 1  \land t
^{(i)} = 1\mid x^{(i)})\\
&=& p(y^{(i)} = 1 \mid x^{(i)}) p(t^{(i)} = 1 \mid x^{(i)})\\
&=& \alpha p(t^{(i)} = 1 \mid x^{(i)})

\end{eqnarray*}
\end{answer}
